About ACSA

Welcome to the Advanced Computer System Architecture Laboratory. Our research seeks to define the architectures that will shape the future of computing. Our goal is to improve the performance, programmability, reliability, and energy efficiency of the next-generation computers.

Join Us!

Thanks for your interest!
We are always eager to have energetic and passionate researchers (postdocs, graduate students, undergraduates, and research associates) join us!
If you are interested in joining the lab, please send your CV and a short description of your research interests to us!
  • If you're interested in Computer Science!
  • If you're interested in High Performance Computing!
  • If you'd like to get in touch with the most powerful machines in the world!
  • If you want to keep learning and keep growing!
